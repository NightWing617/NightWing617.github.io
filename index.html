About Our Salon

Founded by Penny Marshall, our boutique salon brings over 15 years of expertise in aesthetic and beauty treatments to the heart of Pietermaritzburg. As a fully accredited beauty specialist, Penny has cultivated a sanctuary where luxury meets exceptional service.

We pride ourselves on merging timeless beauty traditions with cutting-edge techniques, using only premium, dermatologically-approved products. Our intimate salon setting ensures each client receives personalized attention in a tranquil, professional environment.

With ongoing training in the latest international methodologies, we offer treatments that are both innovative and results-driven, while maintaining that personal touch that makes every visit feel special.
